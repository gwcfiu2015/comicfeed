Antman



About

Scott Lang (Paul Rudd), a master thief or “burglar”, is just released from prison and tries to redeem himself and fix his relations with his daughter. But since life is hard for an ex-convict, Scott Lang finds himself mistakenly becoming the new Ant-man when he steals a super-suit. With his new mentor Hank Pym (Michael Douglas, a.k.a. the first Ant-man), he learns to control this super-suit that allows him to shrink, possess superhuman strength and control an army of ants. The rookie hero must use his newfound skills to stop Cross (Corey Stoll), who becomes the Yellow Jacket, from replicating the same shrinkage technology discovered by Hank Pym. Scott must use his shrinking abilities to stop this newborn supervillain from selling dangerous, life-threatening technology to clients with malicious intentions of war.
