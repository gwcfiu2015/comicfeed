ABOUT US

ComicFeed is a place where you can find out about the latest news in the superhero world. Our group focuses on bringing you the best, most interesting, and up-to-date information that is happening in the superhero world. Whether you're a hardcore fan or it's just an interest, ComicFeed is a place where any fan can find news on the latest movie and TV show adaptations as well as comics. Our group consists of four students from Girls Who Code, a program that supports young girls that have an interest in computer science. Inspired by our love for comics, ComicFeed is a project where we can dedicate this love and we hope that you, the user, will take great joy in using our product.
